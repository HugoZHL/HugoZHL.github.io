General Form of Noether Current

There are two types of transformation that we normally consider:
(i) the more fundamental one: tranformation on the coordinate;
(ii) the other one: transformation on the field. It’s obvious that: every tranformation on the coordinate leads to the corresponding tranformation on the field. Thus, the level of tranformation on the field is higher.
In this section, we derive a general form of Noether current. Consider a general tranformation of the field $\phi(x)$:
$ \phi(x) \rightarrow \phi(x)’ = \phi(x)+\alpha \Delta \phi(x) $
